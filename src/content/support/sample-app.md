## Welcome to Sample App Support

Thank you for using Sample App. This page provides support information and answers to frequently asked questions.

## FAQ

### How do I get started?
After downloading the app, simply open it and follow the on-screen instructions to set up your account.

### The app is not working properly. What should I do?
1. Make sure you have the latest version of the app installed
2. Try restarting the app
3. If the issue persists, please contact us using the information below

### How do I delete my data?
You can delete your data from the Settings menu within the app. If you need further assistance, please contact us.
